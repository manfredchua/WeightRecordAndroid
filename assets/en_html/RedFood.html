Avoid

Eat your food slowly to recognize when you feel full faster and avoid overeating.

Protein from Meat Sources

  • Regular hamburger (73% lean)
  • Processed chicken (e.g. chicken fingers)
  • Fatty meats (sausage, marbled beef, bacon)

    • Protein from Non- Meat Sources

  • Sweetened yogurt
  • Protein shake mixed with fruit juice
  • Full fat cottage cheese
  • Whole milk (4% milk fat)
  • Excessive cheese
  • Sour cream

    • Grains, Legumes, Whole Grain Bread, Nuts

  • White bread
  • Club crackers
  • White rice
  • High sodium canned legumes
  • Candied nuts, macadamia nuts

    • Fruits and Vegetables

  • Fruit juices, dates, sweetened dried fruits, canned fruit in syrup
  • Sweetened applesauce
  • Mashed potatoes with butter
  • Vegetables with sauce, especially cream sauce

    • Others

  • Corn oil, margarine, hydrogenated oils, lard, shortening
  • Pastries, candy, milk/ white chocolate
  • Sugar
  • Pre-sweetened drinks, fruit drinks, fruit juices, soda
